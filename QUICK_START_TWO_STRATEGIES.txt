================================================================================
                           HOW TO USE BOTH
================================================================================

STEP 1: CHECK BOTH PREDICTIONS
  ML says: Number 1, confidence 45%
  Frequency says: Number 9, confidence 62%

STEP 2: COMPARE
  ❌ They disagree → SKIP (high risk)
  ✅ They agree → Consider betting

STEP 3: CHECK CONFIDENCE
  ❌ Both < 55% → SKIP (too uncertain)
  ✅ Both > 60% → BET (good odds)

STEP 4: DECIDE
  If both agree AND both > 60% → Bet
  Otherwise → Wait for next round

EXAMPLE: Good Betting Signal
  ML: Number 5, confidence 72%
  Freq: Number 5, confidence 68%
  → Both agree on 5, both high confidence → BET 5

EXAMPLE: Skip This Round
  ML: Number 3, confidence 48%
  Freq: Number 7, confidence 55%
  → Different predictions, low confidence → SKIP
